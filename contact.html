Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Are you accepting commissions?

We are currently not accepting new restoration commissions. We hope to reopen this in the future — please check back or send us an email to be notified.

Can I donate a photograph?

Yes — we gratefully accept donations of Victorian and Edwardian photographs for the archive. Please send us an email with a description and, if possible, a scan or photograph of the image.

What kind of enquiries do you answer?

We are happy to help with historical context, dating photographs, identifying photographic processes, and general questions about Victorian and Edwardian photography.

Can you identify people in photographs?

We cannot promise identification, but can often provide historical context — photographic process, date, studio information, and census research.
